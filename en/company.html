NAVESTRIAS EHS is a Consultant firm in Environment, Health and Safety (EHS)

We offer a suitable answer, with common sense and with accessible values in these services that help our clients to fulfill the regulatory legal requirements and simultaneously to maintain a safe and productive place of work.

We attend in the simple subjects that require fast resolution or we can collaborate of internal way in the organization of the Health, Safety and Environment area, eliminating expenses of complete time personnel.

We provide experience for the identification, evaluation, control and reduction of the Industrial and Environmental Risks.
 
NAVESTRIAS EHS controls the chemical, biological and physical Risks, as much in the place of in environment associated work like.
NAVESTRIAS EHS works for Employees and Employers in:
...
º Providing cost effective design solutions to minimize or eliminate hazards.
º Establishing procedures to increase workplace safety and improve human comfort
º Complying with environmental and occupational safety and health regulations.
º Providing "good faith" and "due diligence" surveys.
