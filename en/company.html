About NAVESTRIAS EHS

NAVESTRIAS EHS is an Environmental, Health and Safety (EHS) consulting firm.

We specialize in resolving EHS challenges faced by organizations and companies in both the private and public sectors.

We offer appropriate solutions based on common sense and accessible values in services that help our clients meet legal regulatory requirements while simultaneously maintaining a safe and productive workplace.

We assist with simple matters requiring quick resolution or can collaborate internally with the organization in the Health, Safety and Environment area, eliminating full-time personnel expenses.

We provide expertise for the identification, evaluation, control and reduction of Industrial and Environmental Risks.

Our Services Include

Environmental Services

  • Facilities and Site Investigations
  • Hazardous Materials Management
  • Spill Management and Prevention
  • Risk Reduction Plans with Associated Costs
  • Site Remediation
  • Contractor Monitoring
  • Employee Training
  • Applicable Legal Permits
  • Environmental Restoration

Risk Monitoring

NAVESTRIAS EHS monitors chemical, biological and physical risks, both in the workplace and in the associated environment.

We Work with Employers and Employees to:

  • Provide cost-effective solutions to minimize or eliminate risks and hazards
  • Establish procedures to increase industrial safety in the workplace and increase human comfort
  • Ensure compliance with Hygiene, Workplace Safety and Environmental Regulations
  • Provide good faith and due diligence assessments

NAVESTRIAS EHS has Project Managers such as Chemical Engineers, Occupational Health and Safety Specialists, and Environmental Specialists.
