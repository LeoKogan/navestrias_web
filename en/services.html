Environmental Site Assessment

In support of property or industrial facility transfers, NAVESTRIAS EHS performs Phase 1 and Phase 2 Environmental Site Assessments to determine if land, water or buildings are contaminated. Our environmental team will conduct investigations quickly and diligently.

Phase 1

Phase 1 is a recommended action that the buyer can take to learn about the history of the property and its characteristics and whether or not hazardous substances exist. During Phase 1 we investigate Facilities to determine if there are unresolved or concerning legal issues associated with the Environmental or Health & Safety condition.

  • Review of site files
  • Site visit and inspection
  • Personal interviews
  • Report

Phase 2

During Phase 2, we determine the potential level of risks through sampling and laboratory analysis of already identified elements such as:

  • Construction Materials
  • Chemical products
  • Air, soils
  • Groundwater
  • Surface water

OHSAS 18001 - Implementation of Occupational Safety and Health Management System

What is OHSAS 18001?

OHSAS 18001 is an international standard that provides requirements related to Occupational Safety and Health Management Systems. This enables an Organization to have knowledge and control of all relevant hazards, resulting from normal or abnormal operation, and to continuously improve its performance.

What are the benefits of implementing OHSAS 18001?

  • Improves efficiency and consequently reduces accidents and lost production time
  • Increases control and reduction of hazards through setting objectives, targets and associated responsibility
  • Demonstrate commitment to personnel, property and plant protection
  • Demonstrate compliance with legislation
  • Increase the organization's reputation for Occupational Safety and Health
  • Reduction of occupational insurance costs
  • As part of the sustainable strategy
  • Ensure more effective internal and external communication
  • Improve Safety culture
